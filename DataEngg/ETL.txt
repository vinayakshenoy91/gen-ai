ETL:
----    
- extract, transform (done in staging) and loading
- Large volumes of raw data from multiple sources, converts it for analysis and loads that data into warehouse
- Staging area acts as a buffer between the DW and the source data.
It is used for data clensing and organisation.A big challenge during the data extraction process
is how your ETL tool handles structured and unstructured data.

Transformation:
---------------
- Data cleaning and org stge is the transformation stage
- Data from multiple soruces is normalised and converted to a single system format.
- Methods in transformation: Cleaning, Filtering, Joining, Sorting, splitting, dedup, summarization
- Data extracted to staging area and transformed data is loaded into DW

- Oracle data integrator
